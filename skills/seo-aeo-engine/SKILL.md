# seo-aeo-engine

## Purpose
Use this skill for search optimization and AI-answer optimization.

## What to do
- Write clear, keyword-rich headings.
- Add strong meta title and meta description.
- Include FAQ content in question-and-answer form.
- Add structured data for Organization, LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, and Breadcrumb.
- Support local SEO with service areas, contact details, and trust signals.
- Make content easy for AI systems to summarize accurately.

## Good output
- Search-friendly content
- Direct answers
- Schema coverage
- Local relevance
- GEO-friendly wording
