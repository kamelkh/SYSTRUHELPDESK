# brainstorming

## Purpose
Use this skill before starting new features, major rewrites, or uncertain changes.

## What to do
- Define the objective in one sentence.
- List constraints, assumptions, and success criteria.
- Identify the smallest useful next step.
- Prefer falsifiable hypotheses over broad exploration.
- Keep the plan short and actionable.

## Good output
- Clear scope
- Known risks
- Next action
- Validation idea
